Grilled Fish in Garlic Butter Sauce

Freshly Grilled Fish

Description

Ingredients

  • 500 gm fresh fish fillets of choice
  • 2 tablespoon parsley salt as required
  • 9 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice black pepper as required
  • 175 gm salted butter

Steps

  1. Place a pan over medium flame and sprinkle some olive oil in it.
  2. Once the oil is hot, place the fish fillets in it. Let the fillets cook and sprinkle salt, and black pepper and add lemon juice over it.
  3. Grill it on both sides and once the fillets turn crispy, transfer them on a plate.
  4. For the sauce, place a pan over medium flame and add cloves in it.
  5. Roast them lightly and then transfer them to a grinder jar. Grind them to form a coarse powdered mixture.
  6. Place another pan over medium flame and add butter along with chopped garlic to it.
  7. Saute these well until the butter melts and then add ground cloves, remaining sugar along with salt and black pepper powder over it. Mix it well until the mixture turns aromatic.
  8. Pour the garlic butter sauce over the grilled fish
  9. emove the sauce from the flame, pour over the grilled fish fillets.
  10. Garnish with chopped parsley and rosemary.
