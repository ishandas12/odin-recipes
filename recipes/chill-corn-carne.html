Chilli Corn Carne

A Classic Mexican Chilli

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g lean minced beef (10% or less fat)
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, peeled and ﬁnely chopped
  • 1 to 2 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 150ml red wine or extra stock
  • 300ml beef stock, made with 1 beef stock cube
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can of red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ﬂaked sea salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place a large non-stick saucepan over a medium heat and add the beef and onions.
  2. Cook together for 5 minutes, stirring the beef and squishing it against the sides of the pan to break up the lumps.
  3. Add the garlic, 1 to 2 teaspoons of chilli powder, depending on how hot you like your chilli, and the cumin and coriander.
  4. Fry together for 1 to 2 minutes more.
  5. Slowly add the wine and then the stock, stirring constantly.
  6. Season with a pinch of salt and plenty of freshly ground black pepper.
  7. Bring to a simmer on the hob, then cover loosely with a lid.
  8. Reduce the heat and leave to simmer gently for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally until the mince is tender and the sauce is thick.
  9. Adjust the seasoning to taste and serve.
