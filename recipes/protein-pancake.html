Protein Pancake

A Guilt-Free and Healthy Pancake

Description

Ingredients

  • One scoop of vanilla protein powder
  • Half a cup oat flour
  • Half a teaspoon of baking powder
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 2 eggs
  • Half a cup of almond milk
  • Half a cup of fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Mix some oat flour with protein powder. Add some lemon zest to it. Baking powder is alos a must here.
  2. Add the lemon juice, Greek yogurt, eggs, and almond milk to the dry mixture. Mix until well combined.
  3. Now you can fold the fresh blueberries.
  4. Next, it's time to cook the pancakes on low heat.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Serve with a sprinkle of lemon zest, additional blueberries, and a drizzle of honey.
